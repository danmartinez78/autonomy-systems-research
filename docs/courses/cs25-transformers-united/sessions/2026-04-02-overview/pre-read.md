## Questions for Instructors

1. How do you see the relationship between attention mechanisms and reasoning capabilities evolving?
2. What are the most promising directions for making transformers more sample-efficient for robotics applications?
3. How can we better understand what transformers are actually learning vs. just memorizing patterns?
4. What role does world simulation play in developing embodied AI systems?
5. How do we balance the trade-off between model scale and inference speed for real-time robotics applications?
